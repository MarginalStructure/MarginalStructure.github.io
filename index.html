My work now

I'm a MD-PhD student studying clinical epidemiology at the Institute for Health Policy, Management, and Evaluation at the University of Toronto.

Most of the time I'm located at the Centre for Urban Health Solutions at St. Michael's Hospital, working with my supervisor Dr. Gillian Booth and the rest of my fantastic colleagues. I'm also affiliated with the Institute for Clinical and Evaluative Sciences. Finally, I'm indebted to the great group at the Spatial Analysis of Urban Systems (SAUSy) Lab who let me pretend to be a geographer on occasion.

Before that

I completed my B.Sc. in Psychology at the University of Toronto, leading to a M.Sc. in Medical Science/Neuroscience also at U of T.
